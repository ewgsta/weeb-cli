## What is Weeb CLI?

A terminal-based anime streaming and downloading tool built with Python. It supports popular platforms like HiAnime, AllAnime, Animecix, Turkanime, and Anizle.

## Key Features

**Multiple Sources**: Turkish (Animecix, Turkanime, Anizle) and English (HiAnime, AllAnime) sources.
**Instant Streaming**: Watch in high quality instantly with MPV player, no waiting for downloads.
**Resume Playback**: Automatically saves your progress down to the exact timestamp.
**Smart Downloads**: Fast multi-connection downloads with Aria2, queue system, concurrent downloads.
**Local Library**: Scans your downloaded anime, supports external drives, offline search.
**Notifications**: Get system notifications when downloads complete.
